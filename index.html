Project Name: Choco Berry Surprise

Project Description

Strawberries are often dipped in chocolate as a Valentines Day tradition in the U.S. Strawberries are also served with Chocolate fountains for dipping, often on wood skewers. Chocolate fondue is also eater with various fruits for dessert. This project is designed solely to recognize all of the ways to sell, advertise, and reproduce this product for commercial sale. With that as our main objective we have carefully crafted various tests help see what the consumer's needs and tastes are in this industry. A total of 6 tests will be comprised in this project through the use of Usability Hub. Along with the test links there will be a corresponding link that will show the results of the test as well to better accomodate the users.
